Double Suite

A double suite room is a type of accommodation that typically consists of two separate rooms, including a bedroom and a living area. It is designed to provide comfort and privacy for two individuals or a small group. The bedroom typically features a double bed or two twin beds, while the living area may include a seating area, a dining table, and sometimes even a kitchenette.
